## What is Easy Mode?

Easy Mode is Cliper Tube's one-click automated workflow. Instead of manually controlling every step, you configure your content goals upfront and let the AI handle the rest.

## The Easy Mode Pipeline

```
YouTube URL ‚Üí Configure ‚Üí Download ‚Üí Analyze ‚Üí Clip ‚Üí Caption ‚Üí Voice Over ‚Üí Stitch ‚Üí Export
```

Each step is automated based on your configuration.

## Configuration Options

### 1. Content Purpose

**Why are you creating this content?**

| Purpose | Best For | Optimizations |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| **Selling Product** | E-commerce, product reviews | Persuasive tone, product CTAs |
| **Channel Growth** | New creators, subscriber goals | Friendly tone, subscribe CTAs |
| **Engagement/Views** | Viral content, entertainment | Dramatic hooks, engagement CTAs |
| **Affiliate Marketing** | Commission-based promotion | Enthusiastic tone, link CTAs |
| **Brand Awareness** | Company/personal branding | Professional tone, brand CTAs |

### 2. Engagement Style

**Why will people watch your clip?**

| Style | Hook Examples | Best For |
|-------|---------------|----------|
| **Drama/Controversy** | "You won't believe..." | Reaction content, debates |
| **Life Event** | "This moment changed..." | Emotional content, milestones |
| **Breaking News** | "Just announced..." | Time-sensitive content |
| **New Drop** | "Finally here..." | Product launches, releases |
| **Tutorial** | "Here's how..." | Educational content |
| **Reaction** | "My honest reaction..." | Commentary, reviews |
| **Motivation** | "This changed my life..." | Inspirational content |
| **Entertainment** | "Watch this..." | Fun, shareable moments |

### 3. AI Voice Over

**Configure your narration:**

- **Enable/Disable**: Toggle voice over on or off
- **Voice Tone**: Choose from 8 options
  - Dramatic, Enthusiastic, Persuasive
  - Friendly, Professional, Urgent
  - Calm, Energetic
- **Include Hook**: Auto-generate opening line
- **Include CTA**: Auto-generate call-to-action
- **Custom Script**: Override with your own text

### 4. Affiliate Info (Optional)

For Selling/Affiliate purposes:

- **Platform**: Amazon, Shopify, ClickBank, Custom
- **Product Name**: What you're promoting
- **Affiliate Tag**: Your affiliate ID
- **Price**: Product price
- **Call to Action**: "Link in bio!", etc.

### 5. Quick Settings

- **Platform**: YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, X, LinkedIn
- **Caption Style**: Cinematic, Punch, Minimal, Brand Kit
- **Max Clips**: 1-12 clips to generate

## Using Easy Mode

### Step 1: Paste URL

Enter a YouTube video URL. Supported formats:
- `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
- `https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID`
- `https://www.youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID`

### Step 2: Configure

1. Select your **Content Purpose**
2. Choose your **Engagement Style**
3. Configure **AI Voice Over** (recommended: ON)
4. Set **Platform** and **Caption Style**
5. Adjust **Max Clips** if needed

### Step 3: Start Pipeline

Click the **"Create [Purpose] Video"** button.

## Tips for Best Results

### Choosing Purpose
- Be honest about your goal
- The AI optimizes differently for each purpose
- Selling requires product info filled in

### Choosing Style
- Match the source video's energy
- Drama works for debates, arguments
- Tutorial works for how-to content

### Voice Over Settings
- Enable Hook for attention-grabbing intros
- Enable CTA for action-driving outros
- Use Custom Script for specific messaging

### Affiliate Content
- Always fill in product details
- Include price for urgency
- Keep CTA short and memorable

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid YouTube URL"
- Check the URL format
- Ensure video is public
- Try a different video

### Pipeline Fails at Download
- Check internet connection
- Video may be region-locked
- Try again in a few minutes

### Voice Over Not Generated
- Ensure voice over is enabled
- Check system voice availability
- Try a different tone

### Export Quality Issues
- Adjust platform setting
- Ensure source video is high quality
- Check available disk space
